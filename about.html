About Me

Hey! That’s me

I am Narendra Nath Banerjee, currently here at my 6th Semester at Dept. of Economics, Jadavpur University.

My particular areas of interest are macroeconomic trade principles and the geopolitical tensions that both arise due to them and drive it. Do I know much about it? Most probably no, but I am hoping to get there one day.

I enjoy game theoretic problems and logical puzzles. I've lost touch with them lately, but a good TED-Ed video has a way of pulling me back in. If you have an interesting puzzle that needs some brainstorming do feel free to bring them to me.

I'm trying to get better at case studies. Emphasis on trying.

I've always been fascinated by mythology — not the popular stuff, but the lesser known ones. The way I see it, myths are just the world's first attempts at storytelling, and every culture was asking the same questions with different gods. If you know an obscure one, do entertain me.

Other than that, I occasionally write fiction, my way of sharing random things that have happened throughout the day to no one in particular. Have had this idea of a novel for like 8 years or more now. Will try to finish it eventually.

I occasionally present research at academic forums (quite successfully to my surprise) and once organized an economics meet that I'm still recovering from.

Oh and I game, quite a few famous mobile ones — Clash of Clans, Clash Royale, eFootball and Monoposto, console gaming when I save up enough to buy those.
